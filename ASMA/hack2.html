Matte Lipstick

Too glossy and patchy lipstick? Just place a tissue paper lightly on your lips and apply some loose powder on it with a fliffy blush. Enjoy your matte lips.

Avoid stains

Lip paints are for your lips not for your teeth. After applying your favourite lipstick do the most common fingure test. And your teeth are safe.

Leftovers

Running out of your favourite lipstick? Take the leftovers out of the lipstick bottle and put it inside a eyeshadow pan. If needed you can melt it as well. Apply it with a lip paint brush.

Broken Lipstick

What to do with a broken lipstick?! Place the broken part on your lipstick and heat it with a lighter. Leave it to dry and it is perfectly ready to use.
