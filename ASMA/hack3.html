Makeup Remover

A surprising fact is coconut oil can be a very good natural makeup remover. Keep your skin safe from harmful chemicals using coconut oil and thank me later

Exfoliator

Don't waste your money buying expensive exfoliators. Just dip a lemon slice on sugar and gently rub on your lips. Don't be too rough.

DIY Facepack

For glowing and spot free skin thik hack is a must. Mix a tea spoon of honey and a tea spoon of turmeric in a bowl. Apply this finely mixed paste twice in a week. You can store it in your freezer for 2 days at best.

DIY Toner

Take a spray bottle full of water. Pour blended aloe vera gel inside the bottle and shake it well. Your own toner is ready. You can store it for a week.
